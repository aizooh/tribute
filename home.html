Tribute to Pastor Jason Kamotho

Pastor Jason Kamotho

Greetings! I would like to introduce you to Pastor Jason Kamotho, a dedicated servant of God who leads the congregation at Eagles Chapel Kitengela. The church is situated along Nairobi-Namanga Road.

Pastor Jason was born on August 23, 1981. This date marks not only his birth but the beginning of a life committed to spreading the teachings of faith and love within the community.

Join us in celebrating the life and ministry of Pastor Jason Kamotho at Eagles Chapel Kitengela!

Pastor Jason Kamotho is a guiding light in our lives and a true shepherd of our spiritual community. He has dedicated his life to spreading the message of love, compassion, and faith.

Throughout his tenure, Pastor Kamotho has shown an unwavering commitment to The Potters house. He has not only led us in prayer but also provided a source of strength, wisdom, and support during both joyful and challenging times.

One of the qualities that make Pastor Kamotho exceptional is his wisdom, which has inspired us to become better individuals. His teachings resonate with warmth and sincerity, creating a sense of unity within our congregation.

This tribute is a token of our gratitude for the tireless efforts, compassion, and leadership that Pastor has shared with us. He have left an indelible mark on our hearts and souls, and we are blessed to have him as our spiritual guide.
