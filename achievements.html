06th November 2021
Ordination Ceremony.

On November 6, 2021, an auspicious and transformative moment unfolded in the spiritual journey of Pastor Jason Kamotho at Eagles Chapel Kitengela.On this significant day, Pastor Jason underwent a remarkable transition, moving from the esteemed title of Pastor to the revered designation of Reverend.
The ordination ceremony, graced by the presence of the presiding minister, Apostle Dr. John Gacheru, added a profound layer of spiritual significance.
Under the guiding hand of Apostle Dr. John Gacheru, the ceremony was infused with solemnity and reverence. The transition symbolized not only personal growth and commitment for Reverend Jason Kamotho but also marked a momentous elevation in his spiritual leadership role within the congregation.
The presence of Apostle Dr. John Gacheru, as the presiding minister, underscored the solemnity of the occasion and imparted a blessing to the journey ahead.
Reverend Jason Kamotho, carrying the weight of the newly bestowed title, now stands as a beacon of spiritual guidance and stewardship for Eagles Chapel Kitengela. The transition reflects a deepened commitment to his ministry, resonating with the congregation and the wider community. With Apostle Dr. John Gacheru's guidance, this ordination signifies not just a change in title but a continued dedication to the service of faith and community.

05 th February 2023
FIRST SUNDAY AT STAGE 3

O n the momentous 5th day of February in 2023, the Stage 3 congregation experienced a transformative event that became a cornerstone in their collective journey. This particular Sunday marked not only the commencement of their worship on newly acquired land but also inaugurated a new phase in the life of the church. The atmosphere was charged with joy and gratitude as the community gathered to partake in their inaugural service on the sacred grounds they had recently purchased.
As February unfolds, the church is on the brink of commemorating its first anniversary since establishing a spiritual home at Stage 3. The anticipation is high, and the congregation looks back with appreciation at the milestones achieved during this significant period. In celebration of this achievement, the church extends a warm invitation to all – friends, neighbors, and fellow believers – to join in the festivities that mark this noteworthy anniversary. Your presence is eagerly awaited to share in the joy, unity, and spiritual blessings that have flourished within the church since that memorable 5th of February.
