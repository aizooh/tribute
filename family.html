Family Information

In the realm of family, Pastor Jason Kamotho stands as a beacon of love and unity. Tied together by the bonds of matrimony, Jason is married to his devoted and supportive wife. Their marital journey is a testament to commitment, partnership, and the enduring strength of their union.

The Kamotho family embodies the essence of togetherness, a source of inspiration for the wider community they serve and a testament to the importance of family values in their lives.
